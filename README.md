# AI Environmental Impact Visualizer

An interactive web application that visualizes the hidden environmental costs of artificial intelligence, including energy consumption, water usage, carbon emissions, and human impact of AI data centers and operations.

[![Live Demo](https://img.shields.io/badge/demo-live-success)](https://jeevannnn.github.io/AI-Data-Center-CNR/)

## What This Shows

- **Energy consumption** of AI queries and model training
- **Water usage** for cooling data centers
- **Carbon footprint** of AI operations
- **Land usage** by hyperscale data centers
- **Human impact** including labor conditions
- **Global distribution** of AI infrastructure with interactive map

## Key Features

- Personal AI usage calculator with real-time impact metrics
- Task cost estimator for individual AI operations
- Interactive world map of 1,136+ data centers
- Case study: Google's Vizag Data Hub in India
- Comprehensive data sources and methodology

## Built With

HTML5 • Tailwind CSS • Chart.js • Leaflet.js • Vanilla JavaScript

## Data Sources

- **IEA** - Energy and AI Report (2024/2025)
- **UC Riverside** - "Making AI Less Thirsty" research
- **Synergy Research Group** - Data center statistics
- **TIME Magazine** - AI labor investigations

## Disclaimer

Educational visualization. Actual impacts vary by model efficiency, location, energy mix, and cooling technology. Estimates based on peer-reviewed research and industry reports.
